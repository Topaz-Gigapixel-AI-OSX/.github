## Overview

<a href="#">Topaz Gigapixel AI</a> redefines what's possible with image enlargement through neural networks trained to synthesize realistic detail. Traditional upscaling merely interpolates pixels, creating blurry enlargements. Gigapixel AI instead generates new detail consistent with image content - texture in fabric, individual hairs, fine text - producing enlargements that appear to have been captured at higher resolution originally.

The <a href="#">AI models</a> within Gigapixel specialize in different content types. Standard mode handles general photography with balanced enhancement. Lines mode preserves architectural edges and geometric patterns. Art & CG mode processes digital artwork and rendered images appropriately. Face Recovery mode reconstructs facial detail in small or degraded faces. Selecting appropriate models produces optimal results for each image type.

<a href="#">Scale factors</a> range from 2x to 6x magnification. Double resolution for moderate improvements or push to 6x for dramatic enlargement. The AI maintains quality across all scales, synthesizing appropriate detail density for the target resolution. Even aggressive enlargement produces usable results when originals have adequate quality.

The <a href="#">Face Recovery</a> feature specifically targets human faces for enhanced reconstruction. Small faces in group shots gain recognizable features. Degraded portraits recover expression and detail. The face-specific neural network understands facial structure to produce natural reconstruction rather than generic sharpening.

<a href="#">Batch processing</a> handles multiple images with consistent settings. Configure enhancement once and apply across folders of images. Queue large batches for overnight processing. Export to specified locations with customizable naming. This batch capability transforms Gigapixel from an editing tool into a production system.

The <a href="#">comparison view</a> displays original and enhanced versions side by side. Drag the divider to examine specific areas. Toggle between versions for full-view comparison. This comparison enables confident evaluation before committing to exports that may take significant processing time.

<a href="#">GPU acceleration</a> leverages graphics hardware for faster processing. Apple Silicon optimization ensures M-series Macs process efficiently. Discrete GPU support accelerates processing on equipped machines. This hardware utilization makes Gigapixel practical for regular use rather than occasional special cases.
